# The Ultimate Guide to Circuit Diagrams

Creating high-quality circuit diagrams is essential for academic reports, component documentation, and hobbyist projects. In this post, we will walk you through the core features of **Circuit Diagram Maker**.

## Drag and Drop

The sidebar features over 40 standard IEC and ANSI symbols. Dragging a component into the canvas automatically snaps it to a 20px orthogonal grid, ensuring everything always stays perfectly aligned.

## Smart Routing

Our custom Manhattan routing algorithm ensures that lines stay perfectly horizontal or vertical.
